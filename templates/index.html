How it works

The scanner uses the four black square markers in each corner of the sheet to correct any tilt or scale variation in the scan, then samples the fill of each bubble against a known template. Standard thresholds: under 25% fill → blank, 25–55% → flagged for review, over 55% → filled. Each row carries a confidence score and a needs_review flag so you can spot-check the ~1% that aren't obvious.

Output columns: serial, roll_number, set, Q1, Q2, …, Q50/Q100, confidence, needs_review, review_items, source_file.

Runs locally on your machine. No data leaves this computer.
